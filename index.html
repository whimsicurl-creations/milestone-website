The Coffee Nook Style Guide

Full Style Guide for New Coffee Shop

To broaden my design skills, I was given the idea for a new coffee shop opening in a local community and tasked with creating a brand identity for it. I decided on the name "The Coffee Nook" and built everything around the idea that this coffee shop would be deeply rooted in the community as a place to gather - whether meeting friends, family, co-workers, or complete strangers.
